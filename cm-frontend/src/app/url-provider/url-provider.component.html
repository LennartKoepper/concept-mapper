Concept Map from URL

Create a concept map from any publicly accesible website (e.g. a wikipedia article) by providing its URL.

The websites do not need to follow a specific structure. Shorter and concise websites will usually yield better results. Choosing an appropriate context preset may also help. Depending on the selected model (and its context size), long websites may result in server-side processing errors, preventing their conversion into a concept map. A method to mitigate such errors is currently under development. However, since there seems to be no direct correlation between the context size and this behavior, it has not yet been possible to determine a specific length up to which websites can be processed without issues.
